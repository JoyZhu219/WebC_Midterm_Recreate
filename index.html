Skiing

Skiing is a popular sport and it is seen as being fast and dangerous. There is something rather attractive about being able to travel effortless down a mountain while being able to appreciate magnificent views.

Skiing for transport, hunting, and war

Skiing was a prehistoric activity; the oldest known skis date to between 8000 and 7000 BCE and were discovered in Russia. Many peoples who lived in climates with snow for many months of the year developed some form of skiing. It has long been employed for military purposes from 15th century.

Skiing for recreation and sport

Skiing both as recreation and as a sport was a natural development from its utilitarian applications. There was competitive skiing in California in the 1860s on straight downhill courses.

OVERVIEW

Skiing is a means of transport using skis to glide on snow

People’s first images of skiing will come from media coverage as few people live in the areas of the world where skiing takes place. The picture often painted by the media is often an attractive one. The people participating are seen as being fit and glamorous and they are kitted out in expensive looking ski suits often accompanied by the most modern of accessories.
